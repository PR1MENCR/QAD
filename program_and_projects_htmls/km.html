QAD KM Handholding Session

Knowledge Management Sessions for SDO Private School Focal Persons, is designed to improve the ability and competency-based method in the management of organization-related information. QAD professionals are expected to facilitate ability and competence that fit within the established standards and thus enhanced areas of the functional deliverable in the Knowledge Management criterion for the craft-benefited view. Similarly, this would aim to improve personnel performance, productivity, and dedication to quality standards as defined and demanded by the DepEd Prime NCR.
