Quality Assurance Division Permit and Recognition Optimizer (QuADPRO)

The Department of Education, National Capital Region, through the Quality Assurance Division has envisioned doing an innovative transaction aligned with the vision of the regional director and as part of trailblazing initiatives in the regional office to achieve effective organization, efficient operation, and economical administration.

In accordance with DepEd Order Number 88 series of 2010, also known as 2010 Revised Manual of Regulations for Private Schools in Basic Education on Government Permit and Recognition, and R A 94 85 or Anti Red Tape Act of 2007, and R A 11032 or Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Government Service Delivery Act of 2018 which ensure the improve efficiency and streamline on the delivery of government services to the public, DepEd NCR introduces QuADPRO.

The QuADPRO is a web application that can be accessed to any web browser. The system is connected to a cloud server which functions as QuADPRO’s data storage.

With QuADPRO, public and private schools applying for government authority to operate will now have direct access to apply and track their application.

From the name itself, QuADPRO was made to optimize the productivity rate of QAD Office on the processing of government permits and recognition. It was an initiative of the regional office to digitally migrate the process through business technology solutions to reduce high volume of tasks, lessen human error and streamline the process. With QuADPRO, a signed government permit or recognition can be available in less than 2 to 3 working days.

The system follows existing QAD standard operating procedures through paperless processing of applications for government permits and recognition.

Other features of the system include level of user accounts and admin approval from Schools Division Offices and Regional Office, School profile and application history, Summary report on ongoing and completed applications through real time Dashboard and email notification to clients on the availability of their government permit.

With QuADPRO as part of Prime Automation, the Legacy of Innovation continues as rich as excellence!
