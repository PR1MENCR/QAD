Quality Assurance Division Permit and Recognition Optimizer (QuADPRO)

The Quality Assurance Division Permit and Recognition Optimizer or QuADPRO was launched in compliance to DepEd ORDER No. 88 series of 2010 also known as “2010 Revised Manual of Regulations for Private Schools in Basic Education”, R.A. 9485 or “Anti Red Tape Act of 2007”, R.A No. 11032 also known as “An Act Promoting Ease of Doing Business and Efficient Delivery of Government Services”, and the principle of Continuous Improvement (CI). Grounded from these bases, it aims to streamline the processes, standardize the operations, provide measurable results, easy tracking, and monitoring, and constitutes high level of transparency and accountability. Through QuADPRO, it boosts the efficiency level of the Quality Assurance Division office as a regulating unit in the Department of Education – National Capital Region in terms of processing applications for government permits and recognition.
